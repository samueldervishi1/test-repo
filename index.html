Captain Jack Sparrow

Captain Jack Sparrow, portrayed by Johnny Depp, is the protagonist of the Pirates of the Caribbean film series. A cunning and eccentric pirate, known for his wit, resourcefulness, and unpredictable nature.

About Jack Sparrow

Jack Sparrow is a pirate captain always looking for adventure and treasure. He is known for his unique style, dreadlocks, and love for rum. His ship, the Black Pearl, is his most prized possession.
