What is a Sparrow?

Sparrows are a family of small passerine birds, the Passeridae. They are also known as true sparrows, or Old World sparrows, differing from both the American sparrows (Passerellidae) and from a few other birds sharing their name, such as the Java sparrow (Lonchura oryzivora).

Types of Sparrows

  • House Sparrow
  • Eurasian Tree Sparrow
  • Chipping Sparrow

Sparrow Habitats

Sparrows can be found in a variety of habitats, including urban areas, grasslands, and woodlands.
